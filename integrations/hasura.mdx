title: Hasura Cloud

---

## Setup

1. **Generate your Personal Access Token**
   1. Go to [your Hasura account settings](https://cloud.hasura.io/account-settings/access-tokens) and create a new Personal Access Token.
   2. Copy the newly generated token somewhere safe because it will **not** be shown to you again.
2. **Configuration**
   1. Go to [integrations catalog](https://app.envsecrets.com/integrations/catalog) in your envsecrets dashboard and choose "Hasura Cloud."
   2. On the setup/connection page, enter your Hasura Personal Access Token you created above and save the configuration.

## Activation

1. Go to the [integrations](https://app.envsecrets.com/integrations) dashboard in your envsecrets organisation and under "Hasura Cloud" choose "Sync New Environment With Your Hasura Account."
1. In the page that opens, select your envsecrets project, environment and your Hasura project and environment where you wish to push/sync your secrets.
1. Complete and save the form.

## Usage

### Platform

1. Navigate to the environment for which you activated the integration.
1. Click on the "Sync" button.
1. Choose the Hasura project to which you want to sync your secrets.
1. Approve the sync.

### CLI

1. Use the command: `envs sync --env [name-of-your-remote-environment]`
1. Choose the Hasura integration.

<Tip>

Once your secrets are synced, it is recommended you go to your Hasura project and validate the new values.

</Tip>

### Reserved Environment Variables

The following secrets/variables cannot be forward-synced to Hasura Cloud because they are reserved for Hasura's internal requirements:

- HASURA_GRAPHQL_ADMIN_SECRET
- HASURA_GRAPHQL_CORS_DOMAIN
- HASURA_GRAPHQL_DISABLE_CORS
- HASURA_GRAPHQL_ENABLE_CONSOLE
- HASURA_GRAPHQL_WS_READ_COOKIE
